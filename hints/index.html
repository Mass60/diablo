This is a tips section for new players of Diablo, or ones that have played other entires in the series before but wanted to play the original. The


Tip one:Always save when you can



Now, This seems obvious, but here's the thing: This game doesn't have autosave, so if you just go ahead expecting to have you progress saved, and then you die, you will go all the way back to the town, and have to backtrack all the way to where you were before. This is not a good thing, so always save, and not just when you are at low health or before aboss: whenever you have free time, reload.




Tip two: AVOID THE YELLOW ZOMBIES

These yellow zombies are called the Black Death, and if they hit you, you lose one hp point, permenantly. You can get more hp points over the course of the game, but always be careful on any floors these are on (3 to 5), especially if you're melee..

Tip 3:Get Teleport as soon as you can

Teleport is one of the most useful spells in the game, esecially for warriors. It's uses are many and useful, such as teleporting close to ranged enemies as a warrior to not have to approch them yourself and take any hits, teleporting to another room if you're getting overwhelmed,and moving through level quickly.
